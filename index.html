I have always been interested in technology and I have tried to focus on this area throughout my career. My internship at Apple convinced me that I had chosen the right field. I was working with like minded people and was excited about the work I was doing.

Since then I have worked at a startup that introduced me to the world of development. I realised early on that I found ‘coding’ really interesting and wanted to learn more about it.

More recently I came to appreciate the need for challenge in the work I do, and whilst I enjoyed working at Time Out, I found that this was missing. I decided to retrain as a developer to truly contribute to the field I had chosen to be a part of. I have learnt so much already but am excited to carry on learning more for the rest of my career. Working as a developer means being open to learning new skills and working with lots of different kinds of people and I’m looking forward to taking on each new challenge.
